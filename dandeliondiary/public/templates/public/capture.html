Dandelion Diary takes the work out of expense management, leaving you with more free time to do the things you enjoy.

Easily capture your expenses as they happen with instantaneous budget feedback. Need a record of your purchase? Take a picture and attach a receipt. Review, search and add notes to any expense at any time.

Features include:

  • Automatic expense filtering of categories based on prior captures, and geo-location information.
  • Immediate feedback on how much remains in your budget.
  • Ability to have every person (13 years of age or older) in your household track expenses—no additional subscription fees.
  • Digitally capture receipts—no more paper clutter or filing.
  • Make free-form notations for easy expense searching and filtering.

There's much more to come. Dandelion Diary is currently in beta. Sign up and receive our newsletter of upcoming features.

Say goodbye to budgetary guess work and the need to save wadded up, hard to read receipts.
