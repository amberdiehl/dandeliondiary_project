{% trans "About Us" %}

Hi, my name is Amber.

Dandelion Diary is the crazy brain child of me, a middle-aged RV'er, with contributing inspiration and support coming from my wonderful husband, Scot, and beloved fur-child, Barney.

We are new to RV'ing--having started in May of 2015--but we are 'all in', having sold or donated almost everything we own to establish ourselves as full-time RV'ers. We love the friendliness of the RV community, the willing spirit to help, the different pace of life, and the ability to move about and experience all that North America has to offer. While much changed, we have kept our rather almost fanatical obsession with tracking expenses, trying to keep costs down and seeking out where to get the best deal or service, bit of know-how or, well, anything, associated with RV'ing.

We used to track our expenses using Microsoft Excel, saving receipts throughout the month and then I would sit down and enter them all at the end of the month. Since developing Dandelion Diary, I have stopped doing that, entering receipts as I make purchases and Scot does the same! It's fast, easy, and now I have visuals I didn't have before of how well we're doing. Actually, the cool thing is, we both have visuals now regarding how we doing for the month.

My hope is that others will use Dandelion Diary too and, between the magic that can be done with software (I have so many more features and ideas of things I want to do), and the magic of people helping other people through experience and expertise, we can create an amazing online community for RV'ers by RV'ers.

Yours truly,

Amber, Scot, and Barney, too.
