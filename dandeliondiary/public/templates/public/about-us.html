Hi, my name is Amber.

Dandelion Diary is the crazy brain child of me, a woman with a professional background in Information Technology, a passion for software development and home design, and a new found love for recreational vehicles.

Yes, my husband Scot and our beloved fur-child Barney, are new to RV'ing--having bought our first RV in May of 2015--and we are 'all in'. Over the course of just three months, we sold or donated almost everything we own to establish ourselves as full-time RV'ers. In the short period since then, we have discovered the friendliness of the RV community, the willing spirit to help, a different pace of life, and the ability to move about and experience new places. While much in our lives has changed, we have kept our almost fanatical obsession with tracking expenses and keeping costs down by seeking out where to get the best deal or service, or bit of know-how for anything associated with RV'ing.

I used to track our expenses using Microsoft Excel. We would save receipts and then I would sit down and enter them all at the end of the month. Doing this, we had a record of what we were spending. But since I didn't want to have to manually categorize everything, lots of things got lumped--giving us an accurate but imprecise view of expenses.

Since developing Dandelion Diary, I have stopped doing that. Now, I enter receipts as I make purchases and Scot does the same! It's fast, easy, and now we both have more accurate tracking along with really helpful charts and immediate feedback of how well we're doing.

My hope is that others will use Dandelion Diary too and, between the magic that can be done with software (I have so many more features and ideas of things I want to do), and the magic of people helping other people through experience and expertise, we can create an amazing online community that supports the dreams and treks of you and me.

Yours truly,

Amber, Scot, and Barney, too.
